About Me

Autobiography

Hi! My name is Maria Rodriguez, although I prefer to be called "Meli'' (it is short for Melissa, my middle name.) I was born on October 29th, 2002 making me 21 years old this year which I am so excited about. I was born, raised, and still residing in the awfully hot South Texas, sadly I have made it one of my goals to move up North to a place with more pleasant and fair weather. I come from an extended Mexican family that shaped who I am today by constantly emphasizing the importance of family support, respect, hard work, and perseverance.


Education and Work

The latest form of education I completed was my highschool education. I was eligible for a three-year graduation plan that allowed me to undertake my junior and senior courses during the same year, allowing me to graduate in 2020, a year earlier than expected! After high school, the idea of continuing my education was something I was always interested in, but unfortunately never pursued due to personal issues. Throughout my gap years, I started working in the retail industry where I was allowed to gain valuable experiences and build character. During this period, I was learning about myself and started setting goals for my future. This pushed me to get into introductory coding courses online where I learned the fundamentals about programming, discovered a passion for this field, and set my sights on pursuing a career as a full-stack software engineer!
