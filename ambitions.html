Ambitions

the big picture


Why Software Engineering?

My ambitious aspiration to pursue a career as a full-stack software engineer comes from being able to have an impact on people's lives while being able to continue developing my problem-solving and analysis skills. The thought of contributing to the development of a program that helps and provides satisfaction to its users will make me proud. Knowing that the work I put into the program benefits individuals to enhance and make their experience easier is going to be super rewarding to other software engineers and myself. This industry is also full of complex technical challenges that will fulfill me when I am able to provide companies and consumers the necessary solutions to their problems!


Why Coding Temple?

I did a lot of research and spent a lot of time talking to people from Career Karma before I committed to a bootcamp. Before committing to Coding Temple, I was researching every bootcamp including Fullstack Academy, Coding Dojo, DevCode Camp, etc.. but I eventually committed myself and the next 6 months of my life to Coding Temple.

Why?
  • Capped cohort sizes providing an exclusive learning environment.
  • Lifetime career support to help prepare me for my desired tech job.
  • Part-time classes allow me to continue working and learn at the same time.

The Big Picture

I believe no goal is too big to achieve, therefore I have set a lot of goals for myself, some regarding my career while others are just personal goals. In this field, many people will probably end up having the same end goal, working for a big tech company. I am a part of this group of people. I want to be a part of a team of one of the companies I love the most, X (formerly known as Twitter), Spotify, or maybe even NASA. I know these companies are worth billions and people may be doubtful when a possible career for these companies is presented, but I will work every day to make sure I differentiate myself from others and constantly work on my code. Through Coding Temple, I will use their resources to the best of my ability. I will work to enhance my professional knowledge, skills, and experience. I will be the best I can be. I am aware that it will take time and perseverance to reach my goal, but I am so dedicated! One day, I will be living in the city, working as a full-stack developer for one of the companies I love the most knowing that all my hard work did pay off!
